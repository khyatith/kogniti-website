What will kids learn?


To take challenges head-on.

56% of the youth feel that the future of the world is doomed. This is because somewhere in the education system, they are not being taught how to deal with challenges.

In Kogniti, we provided kids with unique challenges everyday to build their habit!

First-principles thinking

Kids will learn how to remove all the assumptions and learn to find out facts.

This is called thinking from first-principles.

Self-efficacy.

Self-efficacy is the belief that we ourselves can affect positive changes in our lives.

Self-efficacy is the biggest skill to be built in the future generation.

Collaboration

No one has ever solved complex problems along.Collaboration is the most important skill that we focus on building at Kogniti.

Children collaborate in teams to solve complex problems.

How are our sessions conducted?

  • Students gather up in our zoom session
  • Students are divided into teams
  • Students play the our custom multi-player game in breakout rooms on zoom
  • After the game, session facilitator will help kids self-reflect in groups
